About Us

FacePy was started as a college project for the facial recognition set-ups. The web-app can help an institute to maintain the curriculum & discipline. The FacePy database contains of PIL cropped images of students, & focuses on identifying the same. Any institution faculty can access the software for help at required moment .This application ease a lot of issues which are faced by faculties & institution in indisplinary situations. At FacePy, it all starts with being EAGER: Ethical, Adaptable,Gritty, Empathetic, and Remarkable. We are deliberate about applying these values to everything we do, supporting our users with compassion and integrity, or thoughtfully creating products that can help people.
